My Favorite Books or Book Series

That I have Read in the Past 2 Years

1. Kagen The Damned Series By Jonathan Maberry

I love the strong fantasy setting mixed with the elements of Lovecraftian horror.


2. The Pine Deep Trilogy By Jonathan Maberry

One of the first series that I started listening to.


3. The Relics Series By Tim Lebbon

I walked into it expecting a new horror novel to listen to and was pleasently surprised by an epic fantasy trilogy


4. The Lesser Dead By Christopher Buehlman

I started with Those Across The River and instantly wanted more. This is by far my favorite book as of now.


5. The Influence By Bentley Little

I love it. This is the book that set me on a quest to read as many creature features as possible


6. FantasticLand By Mike Bockoven

I still stay up late at night thinking about this book
